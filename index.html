About the Firm

Built for companies that want output, not overhead.

Companies in the US, UK, Israel, UAE, and Australia overspend on talent — not because the work demands it, but because they're hiring locally by default. Crovanta Partners finds, hires, and manages equivalent-output professionals in India so you can deploy that budget where it actually compounds.

Crovanta is not a staffing agency and not an EOR. We are operators who have independently built two foreign-owned companies in India from scratch — 157+ hires, full compliance, full infrastructure. Every engagement is senior-led, fully managed, and designed to get your first hire operational in under three weeks.

Beyond managed hiring, Crovanta also offers GTM Strategy, Operations Design, People & Org Design, and Fractional COO/CHRO services — all built around the challenges scaling companies face when expanding their operational footprint.
